Prayers (Salah)

Learn how to perform the five daily prayers, what to recite, common mistakes, and tips for a focused and accepted Salah.

How to Perform a Full Prayer (Salah)

1. Intention (Niyyah)

  • Formulate the intention in your heart for the specific prayer you are about to perform.

2. Takbir al-Ihram (Opening Takbir)

  • Raise both hands and say:
  • اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُAllahu AkbarAllah is the Greatest.

3. Standing (Qiyam)

  • Recite the opening supplication (optional):
  • سُبْحَانَكَ اللَّهُمَّ وَبِحَمْدِكَ...Subhanaka Allahumma wa bihamdika...Glory is to You, O Allah, and praise; blessed is Your Name...
  • Recite Surah Al-Fatiha:
  • الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ ...Alhamdu lillahi rabbil alameen ...All praise is due to Allah, Lord of the worlds ...
  • Recite another surah or verses (e.g., Al-Ikhlas):
  • قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌQul huwa Allahu ahadSay: He is Allah, the One.

4. Bowing (Ruku)

  • Bend at the waist, keeping back and head level.
  • Say three times:
  • سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الْعَظِيمِSubhana Rabbiyal AdheemGlory is to my Lord, the Most Great.

5. Standing after Ruku

  • Stand up straight and say:
  • سَمِعَ اللَّهُ لِمَنْ حَمِدَهُSamia Allahu liman hamidahAllah hears the one who praises Him.
  • Then say:
  • رَبَّنَا لَكَ الْحَمْدُRabbana lakal hamdOur Lord, to You is due all praise.

6. Prostration (Sujud)

  • Go to the ground, touching forehead, nose, palms, knees, and toes.
  • Say three times:
  • سُبْحَانَ رَبِّيَ الأَعْلَىSubhana Rabbiyal A'laGlory is to my Lord, the Most High.

7. Sitting between the two prostrations (Jalsa)

  • Sit and say:
  • رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِيRabbighfir li warhamniMy Lord, forgive me and have mercy on me.

8. Second Prostration (Sujud)

  • Repeat the prostration as before.

9. Final Sitting (Tashahhud)

  • After the last prostration, sit and recite the Tashahhud:
  • التَّحِيَّاتُ لِلَّهِ وَالصَّلَوَاتُ...At-tahiyyatu lillahi was-salawatu wat-tayyibat...All greetings, prayers, and good things are for Allah...
  • Recite Salat Alan-Nabi (salutations upon the Prophet):
  • اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ...Allahumma salli ala Muhammad...O Allah, send prayers upon Muhammad...

10. Ending the Prayer (Tasleem)

  • Turn your head to the right and say:
  • السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ اللَّهِAssalamu alaikum wa rahmatullahPeace and mercy of Allah be upon you.
  • Repeat to the left.

The Five Daily Prayers

Fajr

Dawn to sunrise

The first prayer of the day, performed at dawn.

Rakahs: 2 (obligatory)

More About Salah

Sunnah and Nafl Prayers

Voluntary prayers before/after obligatory ones, and at other times, bring extra reward and help make up for deficiencies.

Friday (Jumu'ah) Prayer

A special congregational prayer held every Friday, replacing Dhuhr. Includes a sermon (khutbah) and is highly emphasized.

Missed Prayers (Qada)

Missed obligatory prayers should be made up as soon as possible.

Tips for Khushu' (Focus)

Pray on time, understand what you recite, minimize distractions, and remember you are standing before Allah.
